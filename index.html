How to Subscribe

1

Tap a Subscribe button above

Your phone or computer will ask which calendar app to open. Tap OK or Open.

2

Confirm the subscription

Your calendar app will show a preview. Tap "Subscribe" or "Add" to confirm. You only do this once.

3

Events appear automatically

London events will show up in your calendar. New events are added each week — your calendar refreshes itself, no action needed.

📱 iPhone / iPad

Tap Subscribe → opens Apple Calendar automatically. Works with iOS 12+.

🤖 Android

Tap Subscribe → opens Google Calendar. New events sync within a few hours.

🖥 Mac / Windows

Tap Subscribe → opens your default calendar app (Apple Calendar, Outlook, etc.).

📅 Google Calendar

On the web: go to Other calendars → Add by URL, then paste a link from the section below.

Subscribe Manually

If the Subscribe button didn't work, copy a link below and paste it into your calendar app's "Add by URL" or "Subscribe by URL" option.
